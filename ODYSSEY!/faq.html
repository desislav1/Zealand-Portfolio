What is the minimum age to travel to space?

• You must be at least 18 to travel alone. Children under 18 years are not allowed to travel without parents/guardians.


Are pets allowed?

• Unfortunately, pets are not allowed on the trip. Specially equipped pet suits and trainings are still under development. For now, we will not risk the health of your beloved animals. Follow the updates here: spaceodyssey.com/pets


How long does the journey take?

• Journey depends on your chosen ticket and season. We offer trips of various lengths, starting from 10 weeks to 6 months. Depending on the length of the trip, we offer different preparation trainings for the space journey. More information about tickets and prices here: spaceodyssey.com/tickets


What are the health requirements?

• Before going into space, you will need to complete a full health check-up, including blood test and medical consultations. Traveling is highly discouraged if you have chronic heart or blood diseases. Here you can print our “Health checklist before space travel” to see more information.


How to prepare myself for space traveling?

• After purchasing tickets, you will receive on your e-mail a schedule with a timetable of special trainings lasting two months before the trip.
